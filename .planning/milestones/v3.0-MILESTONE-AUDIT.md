### Integration Issue: TRIAL-06 Race Condition (MEDIUM)

**Problem:** `CreditResolver::checkTrialExpiry()` auto-assigns Free plan during lazy reset. Sidebar fetches `/api/credits` on mount, triggering the reset. By the time `TrialBanner` evaluates `!user?.plan`, the Free plan is already assigned, making the condition false. The "Trial expired - Upgrade Now" red banner may rarely display.

**Impact:** TRIAL-06 implementation exists and is correct in isolation. The race between lazy reset and banner rendering is an integration-level timing issue.

**Recommendation:** Add `trial_expired` boolean to UserResource, or modify TrialBanner to show expired state when `user.plan?.slug === 'free' && trial_days_left === 0`.

### Cosmetic Issue: PlanCard Button Text (LOW)

PlanCard shows "Upgrade" for all non-current plans, including downgrades. Should say "Downgrade" or "Switch" when selecting a lower tier.
